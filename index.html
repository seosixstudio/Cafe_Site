Our Story

About TakeWake Cafe

Born from a passion for exceptional coffee and genuine connection, TakeWake Cafe has been a cherished neighborhood sanctuary since 2020. What started as a dream between two coffee-loving friends has blossomed into a vibrant community hub where every cup tells a story.

We source the finest Arabica beans from ethical farms around the world, roasting them in-house to bring out their unique character. Our baristas are artisans — each drink is crafted with precision, passion, and a whole lot of love.
